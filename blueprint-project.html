Blueprint Project Designer

For this role, I collaborate with a team of developers to improve the design of a period tracking app for The Period Purse. We were to expand the functionality of the app by adding features requested by the client.

Project Goals

The main goal of this project was to expand the functionality of the app by adding features requested by the client, such as ovulation tracking and an improved UX experience.

My Contributions

As we have only recently begun this project, my contributions have been limited to creating wireframes and mockups for the app. I have also been working on the possible design improvements and new features that we could add to the app. I have also worked on bug testing, and reporting issues to the dev team.
